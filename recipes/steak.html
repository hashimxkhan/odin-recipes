Ingredients

  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 1 ½ tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • ⅓ cup low-sodium soy sauce
  • 1 (1 1/2-pound) flank steak

Steps

  1. Whisk together oil, soy sauce, vinegar, lemon juice, Worcestershire sauce, Dijon mustard, garlic, and pepper for marinade until thoroughly combined. Place steak in a 9x13-inch glass baking dish.
  2. When ready to cook, preheat an outdoor grill for medium-high heat and lightly oil the grate.
  3. Cook steak on the preheated grill for about 5 minutes per side, or to desired doneness.
  4. Remove from the grill and let rest for 5 minutes before slicing and serving.
